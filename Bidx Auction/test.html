Property description

A unique and substantial 859 sq. m (9,246 sq. ft) nine bedroom house situated on a site extending to approximately 11 acres (4.56 hectares). This property requires completion works and has the potential for an array of uses subject to obtaining all necessary planning. 

    Location

    The subject property is located approximately 6km north of Mountrath town and is accessed via the R440 where all amenities and services are available. Further services are available in Portlaoise town approximately 15km to the east of the property. The area is serviced by the M7 and several regional roads.
